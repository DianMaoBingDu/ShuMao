书猫 ShuMao

About

ShuMao (书猫, lit. book cat) is an English-Chinese dictionary website inspired by MDBG.

Unlike most online dictionaries, ShuMao is open-source and does not rely on external network services, allowing it to be self-hosted for privacy or reliability concerns. GitHub
